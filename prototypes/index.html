Knowledge Graph — Prototype Gallery

Five different visualization mechanics for 111 atoms and their connections. Same data, different spatial metaphors. Open each to interact.

Classic

01 — Force Constellation

Standard force-directed graph. Nodes repel, edges attract. Clusters emerge naturally from connection density. Drag nodes, zoom, hover for details.

Stars held in gravitational balance
 Tree Rings

02 — Concentric Rings

Dendrochronology layout. Inner rings = deeper formation (L3). Outer rings = newer knowledge (L1). Toggle between level, domain, and chronological views.

Cross-section of a growing mind
 Golden Ratio

03 — Phyllotaxis Spiral

Golden angle (137.5°) packing — the sunflower pattern. Atom #001 at center, newest at edge. Nature's most efficient arrangement. Subtle breathing animation.

A sunflower head of knowledge
 Organic Clustering

04 — Domain Galaxies

Atoms gravitate toward their domain. Cross-domain atoms orbit between clusters. Nebula halos show domain territory. Drag nodes between galaxies.

Galaxy clusters in a knowledge universe
 Living Network

05 — Mycelium Network

Organic root-like network with curved tendrils and pulsing nutrient flow. High-connection nodes glow as fruiting bodies. The network breathes.

Underground root system bearing fruit
Light Theme

06 — Light Minimal

White background, gray outlines, clean. Nitin Sangwan aesthetic. Nodes are outlined circles, not filled. Toggle edges and labels. Zoom and pan.

Pencil sketch on white paper
 Fractal

07 — Fractal Branching

Radial dendrogram. Pick a root atom — the tree branches outward following connections. Click any node to re-root. Organic curved branches thin at depth.

A tree growing from any root
 Full Ecosystem

08 — Seeds + Atoms Network

445 seeds (outlined) connected to 111 atoms (filled). The full knowledge ecosystem. Filter by type (people, concepts, terms, books, frameworks). 1,285 connections.

The entire garden — roots and fruit
 Personal

09 — Personal Navigation

12 curated nodes, not 111. Light gray background. Monochromatic. Shape-differentiated (triangles, squares, diamonds, pentagons, hexagons). Dual nav: sidebar + graph. Click to expand. A person, not a database.

A home, not a data center
 Portrait

10 — Portrait Graph

The graph IS the portrait. 350+ dots form Kevin's face. 12 interactive nodes sit within the features. Hover dissolves the face into a constellation. The portrait breathes, blinks, sways. Monochromatic + Rams orange accent. Dual nav sidebar.
